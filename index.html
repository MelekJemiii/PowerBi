About Import&Export Analytics

Import&Export Analytics is revolutionizing international trade through the power of artificial intelligence and big data. Since our founding in 2015, we've grown from a small startup to the world's most trusted trade intelligence platform, serving over 50,000 businesses across 195 countries.

We process 2.5 million trade records daily, transforming raw customs data into strategic insights that power smarter business decisions. From Fortune 500 corporations to ambitious startups, our clients rely on us to navigate the complexities of global trade with confidence.

Our Mission

To democratize access to trade intelligence and empower businesses of all sizes to compete successfully in the global marketplace. We believe that every company deserves access to the same level of market insights that were once available only to the largest corporations.

  • Global Expert Network: 200+ trade specialists, data scientists, customs brokers, and logistics professionals across 25 countries
  • Proven Results: Clients report average cost savings of 35% and revenue growth of 45% within the first year of using our platform
